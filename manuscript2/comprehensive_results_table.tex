\item \textbf{Notes:} Two-way fixed effects panel regressions with country and year fixed effects. Dependent variable is log percentage of population using internet. Standard errors clustered at country level in parentheses; p-values in brackets. Significance levels: *** p<0.01, ** p<0.05, * p<0.1. Model 1 is baseline specification with Price$\times$EaP interaction. Models 2-3 estimate separate regressions for EU and EaP countries. Models 4-5 use alternative control variables. Models 6-8 extend sample through 2023 with COVID controls. Implied EaP elasticity calculated as sum of Price coefficient + Price$\times$EaP interaction. Elasticity ratio computed as |EaP elasticity / EU elasticity|. EaP COVID effect calculated as COVID + COVID$\times$EaP. COVID impact ratio computed as |EaP COVID effect / EU COVID effect|. All models include controls for log GDP per capita; Model 1 also includes R\&D expenditure and log secure servers. Data sources: ITU (broadband prices, internet users), World Bank WDI (GDP, R\&D, servers, regulatory quality, education, growth).
